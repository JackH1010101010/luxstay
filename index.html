About LuxStay member pricing

Hotels release a portion of their unsold inventory at net rates — below their public retail price — through Closed User Group (CUG) channels. These rates are legally restricted: they cannot be shown on public pages. They are only available to verified members of gated platforms.

LuxStay operates as a CUG platform. When you join, you gain access to net rates on 5-star hotels that are 10–30% below what Booking.com, Expedia, and Hotels.com show publicly. The same hotel, same room, same dates — just a better price because you're accessing it through a member channel.

Last-minute availability is highest from Wednesday onwards as hotels release unsold rooms for the upcoming weekend. This is when the deepest discounts appear.

Frequently asked questions

About member pricing and how LuxStay works

How much do members actually save? ▼
Members save between 10% and 30% compared to Booking.com public prices on the same hotels and dates. On a £300/night room that's £30–£90 back in your pocket. Savings vary by hotel, date, and availability — the best deals appear last-minute (Wednesday–Thursday for the weekend).
Why can't I see the member price without joining? ▼
Hotel rate parity rules require that below-retail prices are only shown to verified members of closed platforms. Displaying them publicly would breach the hotel's distribution agreement. Joining LuxStay (free, takes 30 seconds) makes you a verified member and unlocks the below-retail rates.
Is membership free? Are there any hidden fees? ▼
Yes, membership is completely free. There are no subscription fees, no booking fees added on top of the rate, and no minimum spend. You pay the member rate for the room — that's it.
Which cities are available? ▼
London, Dubai and Bangkok are live now, with all three showing strong member savings. More cities are being added — if there's a destination you'd like to see, let us know when you join.
Is there a minimum stay? ▼
No. All hotels on LuxStay accept single-night bookings, including Friday-only or Saturday-only check-ins. This is specifically designed for last-minute weekend travellers who want flexibility.

FAQ
Do I need to be a LuxStay member to refer?
No. Anyone can become a partner and share a referral link. You don't need to have booked with LuxStay yourself, though trying the product makes for more authentic recommendations.
Is there a minimum payout threshold?
There's no minimum threshold. If you've generated £1 in commission, it will be paid out in your next monthly statement. We believe in paying what's owed without gatekeeping.
What happens if a booking is cancelled?
Commission is earned on completed, non-cancelled bookings. If a referral cancels their booking before their stay, the corresponding commission is reversed. Cancellations after the stay commences are not reversed.
Can I share my link on social media or a blog?
Yes — that's exactly what it's designed for. Travel bloggers, Instagram accounts, YouTube channels, WhatsApp groups, newsletters — all of it works. If you drive volume, get in touch for a custom rate.
How do I know my referrals are being tracked?
Every click and booking is tracked via a unique identifier in your link. We send a monthly statement itemising every booking by date, hotel, value, and commission. If you ever question a figure, email us and we'll share the raw data.
What if I want to refer business clients or corporate bookers?
Corporate bookings tend to be higher-value, which means higher commissions per booking. Get in touch at jackthughes99@gmail.com and we can discuss a tailored arrangement for B2B referrals.
